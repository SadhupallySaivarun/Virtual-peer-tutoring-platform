About Our Platform

We connect students with learning opportunities and empower organizers to improve education for underprivileged children.

How It Works

1. Student Interaction

Students engage in peer tutoring and take a test.

2. Identifying Schools

After the test, students report struggling schools near them.

3. Contacting Schools

We reach out to these schools to understand their challenges.

4. Organizer Support

Organizers provide resources to improve school infrastructure.

Why This Matters

Millions of children lack proper education due to poor school facilities. With our platform, students and organizers work together to change this.

How You Can Help

  • Students: Register and participate in peer tutoring.
  • Organizers: Provide resources and support underprivileged schools.
  • Donors: Contribute to improving school infrastructure.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the platform?

Students, organizers, and donors who want to improve education access.

How do we identify struggling schools?

Students participating in peer tutoring report schools with poor infrastructure.
